About Us

No fancy stuff. Just honest, delicious food.

We're a small family-owned restaurant right in the heart of Nakuru, grown from a humble kitchen into a place where locals and visitors come together to enjoy authentic Kenyan meals made with love and fresh ingredients from our own backyard and local farmers.

We cook slow so you can enjoy — eat up and leave with a full belly and a happy heart. That's why Kenyan cuisine, done right, holds its own anywhere in the world.
